The Dashboard gives you a quick overview of your finances with charts, metrics, and insights.

## Summary Cards

At the top of the dashboard, you'll see:

- **Total Income** — Sum of all income for the period
- **Total Expenses** — Sum of all expenses for the period
- **Net Balance** — Income minus expenses
- **Transaction Count** — Number of transactions

## Spending by Category

A pie or donut chart showing how your expenses are distributed across categories. Hover over segments to see exact amounts.

## Spending Trends

A line or bar chart showing your spending over time. Useful for identifying patterns and seasonal changes.

## Budget Progress

Visual cards showing each budgeted category with:

- Current spending amount
- Budget limit
- Progress bar
- Percentage used

## Quick Insights

Automated observations about your spending:

- Unusual spending compared to previous periods
- Categories approaching budget limits
- Spending velocity (are you on track for the month?)

## Date Range Selection

Use the date picker to view different time periods:

- This Month
- Last Month
- Last 3 Months
- Last 6 Months
- This Year
- Custom Range

## Dashboard Tips

- Check the dashboard weekly to stay on top of spending
- Use insights to identify areas for improvement
- Compare month-over-month to track progress
